New feature on the Chase page chart ("CHECKING BALANCE · TRAILING 12 MONTHS"). This replaces the old Phase F scope — simpler and more useful.

DESIGN:
- The chart window changes: from "trailing 12 months" to "May 2026 (or current month start, whichever is later) through 12 months forward."
- Pre-today: single line, same as today's checking-balance line. Historical data only — no forecast retroactively, the data isn't reliable enough.
- Today and beyond: TWO lines diverging from today's actual balance:
  * Actual line — extends forward day by day as new Plaid balance points arrive. Starts only at today's balance and grows daily.
  * Forecast line — projects from today's balance using the existing forecast engine (cashSignal.ts / the same projection logic /forecast uses), bucketed weekly (Sun-Sat), through 12 months from today.
- Both lines share the same starting point (today's actual balance), so visually it's a single historical line through today, which splits into two from today forward.
- Resolution: weekly Sun-Sat buckets for both the forecast line and (the chart points of) the actual line. Daily resolution would be too noisy on a 12-month window.

NOT in scope: anything locked-week or Debrief-related on this chart. The Debrief variance lives on /debrief; this chart is a separate at-a-glance "are we on track?" view.
